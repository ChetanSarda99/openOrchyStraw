## What is Memo?
ADHD-first universal note aggregator. One search bar for everything saved across Telegram, Notion, Instagram, Reddit, Twitter, voice memos. AI categorizes into 5 dimensions: Source, Format, Area (8 life areas with distinct colors), Tags, Status. See `docs/core/CATEGORIZATION_SYSTEM.md`.

## Source of Truth
**MemoTheme.swift** — single source of truth for all design tokens. No Figma dependency. Visual direction: Linear, Things 3, Obsidian. No hardcoded values ever.

## Design Principles (CRITICAL)

### ADHD-Friendly
- ONE clear action per screen, progressive disclosure
- 16pt minimum padding, clear hierarchy, max 3-5 visible choices
- No guilt-tripping, no streak pressure, no notification spam

### Anti-Slop (STRICT)
- NO generic gradients, stock imagery, Lorem ipsum
- NO default unmodified iOS65 components, NO rainbow spinners
- NO rainbow confetti — area colors only
- Real preview content: "Pro - $9.99/month", "7-day free trial"

### Micro-interactions
- Spring 250-300ms, dampingRatio 0.7 (standard), 0.6 (celebrations), 0.8 (sheets)
- Loading: skeleton shimmer, NOT spinners
- Success: checkmark + haptic, auto-dismiss 1.5s
- Card press: scale(0.98) spring
- Selection: spring scale + border animation
