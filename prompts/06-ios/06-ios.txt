## What is OrchyStraw?

Multi-agent AI coding orchestration. The core runs on a server/laptop via bash.
Your job is to build a native iOS app that lets CS monitor and control it from his phone.

**Repo:** https://github.com/ChetanSarda99/OrchyStraw

## Tech Stack

- **Language:** Swift 5.9+
- **UI:** SwiftUI
- **Min Target:** iOS 17+
- **Architecture:** MVVM
- **Networking:** URLSession (no Alamofire — keep deps minimal)
- **Notifications:** APNs for cycle completion alerts
