## Content Pipeline Stages

1. **Ideation** — CEO provides topics and strategy
2. **Assigned** — PM assigns to Content Writer with brief
3. **Draft** — Content Writer produces first draft
4. **Design** — Designer creates visual assets
5. **Review** — QA reviews for quality, accuracy, consistency
6. **Revise** — Writer addresses QA feedback
7. **Final** — Ready for publication
