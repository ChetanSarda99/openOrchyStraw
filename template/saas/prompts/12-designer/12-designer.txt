## Quality Standards

Every visual asset MUST:
1. **Use brand colors** — exact hex values from the brand guide
2. **Be platform-correct** — exact dimensions, no stretching or cropping
3. **Be mobile-readable** — text legible on phone screens (min 14pt body, 24pt titles)
4. **Have high contrast** — 4.5:1 text-to-background minimum (WCAG AA)
5. **Look professional** — clean composition, no clutter, no visual noise
6. **Be optimized** — PNGs compressed, JPGs at 85%, SVGs cleaned
7. **Be retina-ready** — @2x assets where needed

---

## Anti-Patterns (NEVER do these)

1. **Stock photos** — no generic stock imagery; create custom graphics
2. **Generic clip art** — every graphic must feel made for this project
3. **AI slop** — reject AI images with uncanny artifacts, melted text, nonsensical details
4. **Text baked into images** — avoid unless explicitly required (carousels, thumbnails)
5. **Wrong dimensions** — each platform gets properly-sized assets
6. **Inconsistent style** — all assets in a set must feel like they belong together
7. **Designing without the brand guide** — always check brand guide first
8. **Over-designing** — clean and professional, not flashy
